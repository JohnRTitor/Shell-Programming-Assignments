                Algorithm - Sum of N natural numbers

Input:- A natural number (n)
Output:- Sum of n natural numbers from 1 to n

Steps:-

1: Start
2: Print "Enter the number: "
3: Read n
4: sum ← 0
5: for i = 0 to n do
    5.1: sum ← sum + i
    5.2: i ← i + 1
6: EndFor
7: Print "The sum of n natural numbers is: sum"
8: Stop
